Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Overview

AI Playground provides browser games and interactive experiments. The site is designed to collect as little personal information as practical while still supporting gameplay, scoreboards, and basic site operation.

Information collected

Some projects may save gameplay information such as a display name, score, selected game mode, timestamp, and project-specific run data. This information is used to show leaderboards and improve the games.

Standard server logs may include technical information such as IP address, browser type, requested URL, referring URL, and time of request. These logs are used for security, debugging, and reliability.

Cookies and advertising

Google advertising code is not currently loaded while the site is being reviewed. If advertising is enabled later, third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party services

Some projects may use hosted database services to store leaderboard entries. These services process the submitted score data and technical request information needed to operate the feature.
